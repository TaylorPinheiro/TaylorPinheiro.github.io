Statistical Self Portrait

❮ ❯

A graphic design project "Statistical Self Portrait" assigned by Scott Ahrens from him class Information design, during my sophmore year, fall semester at University Mass. Dartmouth. We were to collect data from our selves in a week's time, make some sort of conclusion or observation from our data and present it in a poster format. Our presented data had to show somthig positive and negative. For example I did "Productivity" for my positive and "Lack of breaks" for my negative. Project created using Adobe Illustrator.
